Description

Welcome to the Web Design Fundamentals Bootcamp (Offline Training), your first step to becoming a professional web designer.


This classroom-based program is perfect for beginners who want to learn how to design and build clean, modern, and responsive websites.


Unlike online tutorials, our offline program offers face-to-face teaching, interactive workshops, and personal mentorship. Even with no prior coding or design experience, you will build confidence to create your own websites.


Here’s why our offline training stands out:

  • Expert-led classes covering design principles and coding basics.
  • Step-by-step practice in HTML, CSS & responsive design.
  • Design principles like color, typography & layouts.
  • Hands-on projects to apply what you learn immediately.
  • Portfolio creation to showcase your design skills.
  • Career support with resume building & mock interviews.
